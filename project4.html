What is Spotify?

Spotify is a digital music, podcast, and video service that gives you access to millions of songs and other content from creators all over the world.

Features

  • Your Library

    You can find your Liked Songs in Playlists and saved albums and followed artists. You can also sort and filter Your Library by choosing a filter at the top or search in your collection.

  • Listen offline

    On Premium, you can download albums, playlists, and podcasts. On free version, you can only download podcasts. You can download as many as 10,000 tracks on each of up to 5 different devices. You need to go online at least once every 30 days to keep your downloads. This is so Spotify can collect play data to compensate artists.
